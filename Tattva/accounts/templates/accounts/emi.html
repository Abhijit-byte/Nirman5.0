How it works

Simple four-step flow for patients and hospitals.

1. Patient selects treatment

Choose hospital and procedure, view indicative cost.

2. LifeCord settles hospital

3. Patient repays in EMIs

Pick tenure and repayment mode: auto-debit or manual.

Add covers for claims and cashless care.
